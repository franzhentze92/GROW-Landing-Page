Who We Are

Nutri-Tech Solutions (NTS) is a pioneering company in the field of regenerative agriculture, with more than 30 years of global impact. Founded in Yandina, Queensland, Australia, NTS has been at the forefront of integrating soil nutrition, biological farming practices, and sustainable technologies into practical farming solutions.

NTS is trusted by farmers, consultants, and agricultural enterprises in over five continents, helping reshape agriculture for healthier soils, stronger crops, and improved profitability.

With a philosophy rooted in Nutrition Farming®, NTS combines science, education, and practical tools to regenerate farmland and empower growers worldwide.
