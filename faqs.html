Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

Find quick answers to common questions about NTS G.R.O.W, from signing up to getting expert support. Need more help? Our team is always here for you!

Guidance

Expert advice and support for your nutrition farming journey

How do I get personalized agronomy advice?
Book one-on-one consultations with Graeme Sait or our expert agronomists through the platform. We offer both scheduled sessions and emergency support for urgent farming decisions.
What makes NTS G.R.O.W different from other platforms?
Our platform combines Graeme Sait's 40+ years of nutrition farming expertise with cutting-edge technology, providing scientifically-backed recommendations that focus on soil health and sustainable practices.
How do I interpret my soil test results?
Upload your soil analysis and our AI-powered system will provide detailed explanations, identify deficiencies, and recommend specific nutrient applications with timing and rates.
Can I get advice for specific crops or regions?
Yes! Our recommendations are tailored to your specific crop type, soil conditions, climate zone, and regional challenges. We consider local factors for optimal results.
How often should I consult with an agronomist?
We recommend seasonal consultations for planning, plus additional sessions during critical growth stages or when facing challenges. Our team is available year-round for support.
What if I disagree with a recommendation?
We encourage open discussion! Our agronomists will explain the reasoning behind recommendations and work with you to find solutions that fit your farming philosophy and constraints.

Resilience

Building robust farming systems that withstand challenges

How can I make my farm more drought-resistant?
Our platform provides strategies for building soil organic matter, improving water retention, selecting drought-tolerant crops, and implementing water-efficient irrigation practices based on your specific conditions.
What practices help with climate variability?
We focus on building resilient soil systems through proper nutrition, crop diversity, cover cropping, and soil health monitoring that adapts to changing weather patterns and extreme events.
How do I recover from crop failures?
Our recovery protocols include soil rehabilitation, nutrient balancing, and strategic replanting strategies. We help you analyze what went wrong and implement corrective measures for future resilience.
Can I get help with pest and disease management?
Yes! We provide integrated pest management strategies that focus on building plant health through proper nutrition, reducing chemical dependency, and creating balanced ecosystems that naturally resist pests.
How do I build long-term soil fertility?
Our approach focuses on sustainable soil building through organic matter management, balanced mineral nutrition, microbial diversity, and practices that enhance soil structure and biological activity.
What about market volatility and economic resilience?
We help you diversify income streams, optimize input costs through better soil management, and build systems that produce consistent quality regardless of external market pressures.

Organisation

Efficient farm management and operational excellence

How do I organize my farm tasks and activities?
Our farm management system helps you plan, schedule, and track all farming activities. Set priorities, assign tasks to team members, and monitor progress with automated reminders and progress tracking.
Can I track my farm's financial performance?
Yes! Track input costs, labor expenses, crop yields, and revenue. Our analytics help you identify profitable practices, optimize resource allocation, and make data-driven decisions for better profitability.
How do I manage my farm records and documentation?
Store all your farm records digitally - soil tests, crop plans, application records, weather data, and compliance documents. Everything is organized, searchable, and accessible from any device.
Can I manage multiple farms or properties?
Absolutely! Manage multiple properties, different crop types, and various farming operations from one dashboard. Compare performance across locations and implement best practices consistently.
How do I coordinate with my farm team?
Assign tasks, share information, and communicate with your team through the platform. Everyone stays updated on schedules, priorities, and important farm information in real-time.
What about compliance and certification requirements?
Track all activities needed for organic certification, food safety compliance, and sustainability standards. Generate reports automatically and maintain audit trails for regulatory requirements.

Wisdom

Continuous learning and knowledge development

What educational content is available on the platform?
Access exclusive videos from Graeme Sait, structured courses on nutrition farming, soil health workshops, crop-specific tutorials, and the latest research in regenerative agriculture - all updated regularly.
How do I stay updated with farming best practices?
Receive weekly insights, seasonal recommendations, and access to our knowledge base. Join webinars, participate in farmer forums, and learn from successful case studies and peer experiences.
Can I learn from other farmers' experiences?
Yes! Connect with a community of nutrition farming practitioners. Share experiences, ask questions, and learn from successful strategies implemented by farmers facing similar challenges and conditions.
How do I access the latest research and innovations?
Our platform curates the latest scientific research, innovative farming techniques, and emerging technologies. Get summaries of complex research and practical applications for your farm.
What about hands-on training and workshops?
Participate in virtual workshops, field days, and hands-on training sessions. Learn practical skills, ask questions in real-time, and get immediate feedback from experts and experienced farmers.
How do I track my learning progress?
Monitor your learning journey with progress tracking, completion certificates, and skill assessments. Set learning goals and track your development in nutrition farming knowledge and practices.
