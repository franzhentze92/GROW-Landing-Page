Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

Find quick answers to common questions about NTS G.R.O.W, from signing up to getting expert support. Need more help? Our team is always here for you!

Guidance

AI-powered support and expert consultations for your nutrition farming journey

How does the AI G-Man chat work?
Our AI-powered G-Man is trained on 30+ years of nutrition farming expertise. Ask questions about soil health, crop nutrition, pest management, or any farming challenge and get instant, scientifically-backed answers 24/7.
How do I book consultations with Graeme Sait or the agronomy team?
Use our integrated booking system to schedule one-on-one consultations with Graeme Sait, our expert agronomists, or support specialists. Choose from available time slots and specify your farming challenges for personalized advice.
What makes NTS G.R.O.W different from other platforms?
Our platform combines Graeme Sait's 30+ years of nutrition farming expertise with cutting-edge AI technology, providing scientifically-backed recommendations that focus on soil health, crop nutrition, and sustainable practices.
Can I get advice for specific crops or regions?
Yes! Our AI and expert recommendations are tailored to your specific crop type, soil conditions, climate zone, and regional challenges. We consider local factors for optimal nutrition farming results.
How often should I consult with an agronomist?
We recommend seasonal consultations for planning, plus additional sessions during critical growth stages or when facing challenges. Our team is available year-round for support, and the AI G-Man provides instant guidance anytime.
What if I need emergency farming advice?
The AI G-Man provides instant emergency support 24/7. For urgent situations requiring human expertise, our support team offers emergency consultation slots and rapid response protocols for critical farming decisions.

Resilience

Advanced soil analysis and crop nutrition tools for robust farming systems

How do I upload and interpret my laboratory analysis?
Simply upload your soil or tissue analysis reports to our platform. Our AI system instantly provides detailed nutrient status overviews, identifies deficiencies, and recommends specific products with application rates and timing.
What crop nutrition tools are available?
Access advanced crop nutrition calculators, deficiency identification tools, nutrient interaction analysis, and crop-specific nutrition programs. Our tools help you optimize plant health and build resilient crops that withstand environmental stresses.
How can I make my farm more drought-resistant?
Our platform provides strategies for building soil organic matter, improving water retention, selecting drought-tolerant crops, and implementing water-efficient irrigation practices based on your specific soil and climate conditions.
Can I get help with pest and disease management?
Yes! We provide integrated pest management strategies that focus on building plant health through proper nutrition, reducing chemical dependency, and creating balanced ecosystems that naturally resist pests and diseases.
How do I build long-term soil fertility?
Our approach focuses on sustainable soil building through organic matter management, balanced mineral nutrition, microbial diversity, and practices that enhance soil structure and biological activity for long-term resilience.
What about market volatility and economic resilience?
We help you diversify income streams, optimize input costs through better soil management, and build systems that produce consistent quality regardless of external market pressures and climate variability.

Organisation

Comprehensive farm management and traceability systems

How do I map and organize my farm fields?
Use our farm mapping tools to create detailed field layouts, track soil variations, and organize your farm spatially. Our system integrates with satellite imagery to provide comprehensive farm visualization and management.
How do I register and track farming activities?
Log all farming activities including planting, fertilization, irrigation, pest management, and harvesting. Our system provides real-time tracking, automated reminders, and comprehensive activity history for each field.
Can I manage my farm inventory and supplies?
Track all farm inputs including seeds, fertilizers, pesticides, and equipment. Monitor usage, set reorder alerts, and maintain detailed records for compliance, cost analysis, and supply chain management.
How does the QR code traceability system work?
Generate unique QR codes for your products that link to comprehensive traceability data. Track your products from farm to market with blockchain verification, providing transparency and building consumer trust.
Can I access satellite imagery for my farm?
Yes! Access high-resolution satellite imagery to monitor crop health, detect stress patterns, and track field conditions over time. Our system provides regular updates and analysis tools for informed decision-making.
What about compliance and certification requirements?
Track all activities needed for organic certification, food safety compliance, and sustainability standards. Generate reports automatically and maintain audit trails for regulatory requirements and certification processes.

Multi-language education and learning resources

What educational content is available in different languages?
Access exclusive videos from Graeme Sait, structured courses, and educational content in multiple languages. Our platform provides nutrition farming education tailored to different regions and farming practices worldwide.
Are there interactive learning games available?
Yes! Learn farming techniques through engaging games, quizzes, and interactive challenges. Our gamified learning approach makes complex nutrition farming concepts accessible and enjoyable for farmers of all experience levels.
Can I access podcast content and blog articles?
Listen to nutrition farming podcasts featuring expert interviews, case studies, and practical advice. Read blog articles covering the latest research, farming techniques, and success stories from the nutrition farming community.
How do I connect with other farmers in the community?
Join our exclusive farming communities to connect with fellow nutrition farming practitioners. Share experiences, ask questions, and learn from successful strategies implemented by farmers facing similar challenges and conditions.
How do I stay updated with farming best practices?

How do I track my learning progress?
