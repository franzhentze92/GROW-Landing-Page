Complete Farm Management System

Run your operation from farms and paddocks through activities, inputs, monitoring, traceability, and reporting—plus Team, Finance, Livestock, and the rest of Organisation.

Farms & Paddocks

Activity Tracking

FARMS, PADDOCKS & SPATIAL PLANNING

Master Data, Maps & Field Layout

Start with farms and paddocks as the foundation—lists, boundaries, zones, and area under management (within your plan’s paddock limits)—then use mapping and planning tools to visualize fields, infrastructure, and seasonal layout. Spatial views make it easier to place activities, inputs, and monitoring where they belong.

ACTIVITIES & PRODUCE

Log Work, Harvests & Outputs

Record planting, treatments, harvests, maintenance, and other jobs against paddocks and farm inputs. Link harvest and produce when you track what left the paddock. Use the calendar to plan and review. This builds operational history for G.R.O.W Reports—your records in the app, not regulatory advice.

SATELLITE, GDD & MONITORING

Monitor Paddocks from Above

Where your plan includes it, use satellite imagery for crop health and change over the season. Pair maps with operational weather and growing degree days (GDD) when those views are enabled. This is remote monitoring—not the same as QR traceability, which covers chain-of-custody in the next block.

LIVESTOCK & AQUACULTURE

Herd, Pond & Tank Operations

Livestock covers herd-oriented records and routines—animals, health, feeding, and related paddock work. Aquaculture supports production units such as ponds or tanks with space for water-quality notes, feeding, and production tracking alongside your other Organisation records. Both sit with cropping in Organisation; full access follows your subscription—see Pricing.

QR TRACEABILITY

Chain-of-Custody & Buyer Stories

Create and manage QR codes that connect batches and lots to the traceability path you run in the platform—from field or shed to hand-off. Use them for internal checks and for the consumer-facing story when you choose to publish it. Separate from satellite monitoring and from generic “compliance” claims.

G.R.O.W VISION

G.R.O.W Vision

Use Organisation’s G.R.O.W Vision suite in the field: Plant ID and Insect ID for quick IDs from photos, Mushroom ID when you need forage or safety context, and Crop Health for image-led checks that sit alongside what you see on the crop.

PLANNING BLENDS, PRODUCTS & SPRAY MATHS

G.R.O.W Smart Tools

Lead with the Cover Crop Blend Builder to design and save mixes, then move through the rest of the toolbox: Irrigation Calculators for water planning, the Product Selector to narrow NTS options, Tank Mix Compatibility before you combine products in the tank, and the Rate Calculator so applied rates stay consistent paddock to paddock.

REPORTING & ANALYTICS

Themed Reports and the G.R.O.W Reports Hub

Work from the classic Report area by theme—planting, harvest, irrigation, fertilization, pest and disease, weeds, operational, administration, and finance—then open G.R.O.W Reports for the farm management analytics hub that pulls irrigation, crop nutrition, crop protection, planting, harvest, produce, and finance into structured operational views.

Comprehensive Organisation Features

From the farm dashboard through fields, inputs, team, harvest, traceability, finance, reporting, production systems, imagery, vision, and smart tools—Organisation keeps operational work connected in one place.

Farm Dashboard

Your operations snapshot in one place: money in and out, activity pulse, and farm performance without diving through menus.

Farms & field operations

Keep farms and paddocks current, log what happens in each field, plan on the calendar, and align activity setup with how you work.

Farm Inputs

Manage inputs on hand, track usage, and build product formulations that stay tied to real applications.

Team & equipment

Run people, payroll, clients, and suppliers alongside machinery registers and maintenance so the crew and kit stay accountable.

Harvest & outputs

Capture harvest events and yield by paddock, then manage batches, specs, and composition for what leaves the gate.

Traceability

Issue and manage QR codes so produce, batches, and compliance stories stay linked from paddock to buyer.

Finance

Record income and expenses against the farm so margins, costs, and cash reality stay visible season to season.

Reporting & analytics

Use themed operational reports plus the G.R.O.W Reports hub for irrigation, nutrition, protection, planting, harvest, produce, and finance.

Livestock & aquaculture

Cover land animals and water production: inventory, health, feeding, growth, movements, harvest, dedicated reports, and dashboards.

Satellite imagery

Watch crop health, local weather, world maps, and growing degree days so field decisions have a view from above.

G.R.O.W Vision

Identify plants, insects, and mushrooms from photos and run crop-health checks when you are out scouting.

G.R.O.W Smart Tools

Plan irrigation, pick products, design cover-crop blends, check tank mixes, and convert rates without leaving Organisation.
